Chat with an anonymous stranger who won't judge you

Connect with someone who will listen

You want to share your feelings, but you can't talk to somebody you know - because let's face it, they might judge you for it or use it against you in the future. CompassionPit is the solution to this: get anonymously connected to another compassionate human from around the world.

Pick an option to the right, and then you will be randomly connected to a chat with an anonymous partner.

There are listeners and venters.

There are venters who need someone to talk to -- please join as a listener.

If you join as a listener and aren't immediately paired up with a venter, your computer will play a sound when a venter joins your chat.

Nobody's online right now, please join in and keep things going!
